Background


Poplings are adorable little popping aliens, created by Cassidy Labs. Cassidy planned to sell four styles of Poplings at toy stores for $3 a piece. My initial role was to research the play patterns. While speaking to people at Cassidy Labs, I also realized that they had questions about how much backstory the toy company needed to provide for children.


Research & findings


CHILDREN OBSERVED

18; 4 via video, 14 in person during play

in natural environment with all four Poplings styles.


FINDINGS FOR CHILDREN

  • Use Poplings as characters, creating their own stories

  • Enjoy popping sound made by the suction cups

  • Engage Poplings with other toys

  • Bounce and throw Poplings


    BIGGEST TAKEAWAY

  • Children love Poplings without a need for much in terms of function or backstory

    PURCHASERS OBSERVED

    Observed: 8 caregivers with purchasing power for children both engage with the Poplings/children.


    FINDINGS FOR PURCHASERS

    • Ask for a story “What are these little guys?”

    • Want high functionality from the suction cups

    • Engage Poplings with each other at a higher rate

    • Sought a price point


      BIGGEST TAKEAWAY

    • Adults, purchasers, seek answers.
